Empowering Entrepreneurs, Transforming Lives

The International Centre for Entrepreneurship and Career Development (ICECD) is dedicated to creating an entrepreneurial ecosystem that empowers individuals—especially the needy—by increasing access to resources, upskilling, and fostering economic, social, and political growth. With a focus on gender equity and sustainable development, ICECD has inspired and impacted millions worldwide.

Our Mission

To create an “Entrepreneurial Ecosystem” by increasing access and control over productive resources, facilitating economic, social, and political empowerment through human resource development, and upskilling beneficiaries in vital life and career skills—with gender issues as a key focus.

  • Human resource development through targeted training and upskilling.
  • Continuous follow-up and tracking of beneficiaries’ progress post-training.
  • Building a global community for shared learning and collaboration.
  • Facilitating access to study materials and entrepreneurship resources for the needy.
  • Promoting gender equity at every stage of the entrepreneurial journey.

Our Global Impact

Community-Based Communication

Active in 55 countries, ICECD connects slum, rural, and urban populations through digital and local initiatives.

Follow-Up for Lasting Success

Tracking and supporting entrepreneurs after training ensures sustainable business growth and resilience.

Inclusive Stakeholder Universe

Engaging nonprofit teams, trainees, and graduates to foster a supportive, collaborative ecosystem.

Measuring Success

ICECD evaluates impact through outreach, stories, testimonies, and organizational profit—driving continuous improvement.
